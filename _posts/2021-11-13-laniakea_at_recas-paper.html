The paper dedicated to Laniakea@ReCaS service has been published on BMC Bioinformatics. The manuscript provides a detalied description of our service, the results of the first year of operations, and presents some of the use cases currently supported on Laniakea@ReCaS.

The paper can be cited as:
Tangaro, M.A., Mandreoli, P., Chiara, M. et al. Laniakea@ReCaS: exploring the potential of customisable Galaxy on-demand instances as a cloud-based service. BMC Bioinformatics 22, 544 (2021). https://doi.org/10.1186/s12859-021-04401-3

We thank all use cases which contributed to our paper.
