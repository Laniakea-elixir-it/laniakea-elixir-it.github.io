Laniakea new web site will be soon available together with its new user interface and reworked encryption system. The latest development iteration of Laniakea introduces a novel and strongly configurable web interface that facilitates a more straightforward customisation of the user experience through human readable YAML syntax and a reworked encryption procedure that exploits Hashicorp Vault as encryption keys management system.
