--- layout: post title: "Laniakea SARS-CoV-2 flavour available" subtitle: "New flavour specifically developed for Covid-19 analysis is available" date: 2020-03-27 012:00:00 -0400 background: '/img/posts/covid19.jpg' pinned: true tag: News ---

A Galaxy covid-19 flavour is now available in Laniakea, as Docker container. It is based [GalaxyProject covid-19 analysis](https://covid19.galaxyproject.org/genomics) and is continuously updated.

Due to the current Covid-19 outbreak, the flavour is already available to users for testing.
