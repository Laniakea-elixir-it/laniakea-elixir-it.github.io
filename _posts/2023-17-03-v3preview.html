During the WALS 2022 conference in Bari, we presented a detailed review of Laniakea Dashboard and encryption moduels. However, that was a precious an opportunity to give a preview of the incoming third version of Laniakea, as: * the possibility to deploy new applications, like RStudio and JupyterLab; * the renewed encryption package pyLUKS * the possibility to create custom credentials for many applications.

A small video demo has been also presented, and is available on our youtube channel.
